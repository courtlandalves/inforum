INFORUM & the Commonwealth Club of California

Since 1903.

INFORUM is the cutting edge programming division of the Commonwealth Club, founded in 1903, the oldest and largest public affairs forum in the United States.

The Club is also the longest running radio program in the nation, broadcast on 230 public radio and commercial stations.

Non-profit and non-partisan, the Club hosts landmark speeches and speakers, from Franklin D. Roosevelt’s “New Deal” speech to Martin Luther King, Jr., Nobel Laureates such as the Dali Lama and Muhammad Yunus, to today’s heads of states and world leaders across fields.

 

INFORUM is the Commonwealth Club experience for a new generation.

The INFORUM division creates interactive events that bring interesting, inquisitive, and connected people even closer to the world's changemakers through deeper conversations.
