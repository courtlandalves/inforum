INFORUM & the Commonwealth Club of California

INFORUM is part of the Commonwealth Club, founded in 1903, the oldest and largest civic forum in the United States. It is also the longest running radio program in the nation, heard across 230 public and commercial radio stations.

The Club hosts landmark talks, from Franklin D. Roosevelt’s “New Deal” speech, to John Muir’s founding of the conservation movement, to Nobel Laureates such as the Dalai Lama and today’s heads of states and world leaders.

The INFORUM division presents new, experimental, exciting, events.

INFORUM is the cutting edge of the Commonwealth Club, for everyone.
